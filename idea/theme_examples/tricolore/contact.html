Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Joining MotoItalia is simple! Fill out the contact form above selecting "Become a Member" as the subject, or visit us at our headquarters in Milano. Annual membership is EUR 50 and includes access to all group rides, workshops, and member discounts with our partners.
Not at all! MotoItalia welcomes riders of all brands and types of motorcycles. Whether you ride a sportbike, cruiser, adventure bike, or scooter, you are welcome. The only requirement is a valid motorcycle license and a passion for riding.
Many of our events are open to everyone, though members enjoy priority registration and discounted rates. Social events and our annual rally welcome all riders. Check individual event pages for specific member vs. non-member pricing.
We organize rides for all skill levels. Each event listing indicates the difficulty (beginner, intermediate, advanced). Our group rides always have experienced road captains and sweep riders to ensure everyone feels safe and comfortable at their own pace.
We welcome partnerships with motorcycle-related brands and local businesses. Please contact us using the form above with "Collaborations" as the subject, and our partnerships team will get back to you within 48 hours with sponsorship packages and opportunities.
