Frequently Asked Questions

Joining Mindful is easy! Fill out the membership form on our contact page or visit us during office hours. We welcome riders of all experience levels. You can also attend one of our open events to get a feel for the community before committing to membership.
Annual membership is EUR 60 per year, which includes access to all club rides, use of club facilities, member discounts on events, and our quarterly newsletter. There is also a family membership option at EUR 90 per year. New members receive a welcome kit with club merchandise.
Our office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. You can also reach us anytime via email at info@mindful.example.
Check our Events page for upcoming activities. Most events are open to both members and non-members, though members enjoy reduced fees or free entry. Simply click the registration link on any event, or contact us directly to reserve your spot. Group rides typically meet at the clubhouse.
For paid events, cancellations made at least 48 hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours receive a 50% refund or credit toward a future event. Membership cancellations can be made at any time, with a pro-rated refund for the remaining months of the year.
