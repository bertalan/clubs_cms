Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about The Society

Membership is open to all who share our values of tradition, excellence, and community. Simply fill out the contact form on this page with "Membership" as the subject, and our membership committee will get in touch with you within 5 business days to guide you through the application process.

Annual membership fees vary depending on the type of membership selected. Individual memberships start at $150 per year, while family memberships begin at $250. Student and senior discounts are available. All memberships include access to our exclusive events, lecture series, and networking opportunities.

Yes, many of our events are open to non-members, though members enjoy preferential pricing and early registration access. Public events such as our Distinguished Lecture Series and Annual Conference welcome all attendees. Members-only events are clearly marked in our events calendar.

We welcome volunteers with open arms. Whether you're interested in event planning, cultural programming, mentorship, or community outreach, there's a place for you. Reach out to us through the contact form and select "General Inquiry" to learn more about our volunteer opportunities.

Yes, The Society currently operates eight chapters across different cities. Each chapter hosts its own local events while also participating in our larger annual gatherings. Contact us to find out if there's a chapter near you, or to inquire about starting a new chapter in your area.
