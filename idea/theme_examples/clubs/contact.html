Frequently Asked Questions

To become a member, fill out the contact form above or visit us during office hours. You will need a valid ID and your motorcycle registration. The annual membership fee is €80 and includes access to all club events, partner discounts, and roadside assistance.

No! EagleRiders welcomes riders of all brands and motorcycle types. What matters is the shared passion for riding and the spirit of brotherhood. Whether you ride a sportbike, cruiser, touring, or adventure bike, you are welcome here.

Some events like Open Days are open to everyone. However, rallies, tours, and most club activities are reserved for members. We recommend attending an Open Day first to get to know us, and then joining if you like the atmosphere!

All members are covered by the club's group insurance during official events. This includes civil liability and basic roadside assistance across Italy. For international tours, supplementary coverage options are available at a discounted group rate.

Members can submit event proposals through the contact form or during our monthly meetings held every first Thursday. The board reviews proposals and, once approved, adds them to the official calendar. We encourage members to take an active role in shaping our activities!
