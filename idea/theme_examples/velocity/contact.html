Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Joining ClubHub is easy! Simply fill out the contact form above or visit our registration page. Annual membership costs €50 and includes full access to all events, workshops, and member-only resources. You can also visit us during office hours.
We organize a wide variety of events including an annual conference, seasonal workshops, monthly meetups, retreats, hackathons, and social gatherings. Check our Events page for the full calendar and upcoming activities.
Absolutely! Some of our events are open to non-members, especially monthly meetups and social gatherings. However, members get priority registration, discounted prices, and access to exclusive member-only events and workshops.
We are always looking for passionate volunteers! You can help with event organization, mentoring, content creation, or community management. Reach out via the contact form and select "General Inquiry" to learn about current volunteer opportunities.
Event registrations can be cancelled up to 72 hours before the event for a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours receive a 50% refund or a credit toward a future event. Membership fees are non-refundable but transferable to another person within the same calendar year.
