About GreenOrg

Cultivating sustainability since 2014

Our Story

Founded in 2014, GreenOrg began as a small group of neighbors who shared a vision of a more sustainable community. What started as weekend garden meetups has grown into a thriving network of environmental advocates.

Today, we work with local governments, schools, and businesses to promote sustainable practices. Our community garden produces over 2,000 pounds of organic vegetables annually, all distributed to local food banks.

We believe that small actions, multiplied by many, create lasting change. Every tree planted, every workshop held, every volunteer hour contributed brings us closer to a greener future.
